# 📝 Posting Guidelines

Welcome to **s3g** — a community-powered gallery platform. Please follow these simple rules to keep the experience enjoyable for everyone.

---

## ✅ Allowed

- Upload original images, artwork, or projects.
- Share creative work you have rights to.
- Tag your uploads appropriately.
- Engage respectfully in comments (if enabled).

---

## 🚫 Not Allowed

- NSFW or offensive content.
- Copyrighted media without permission.
- Spam, advertisements, or self-promo links.
- Multiple accounts to manipulate visibility.

---

## 🔐 Access Levels

| Role              | Can Upload | Can Delete | View All Posts |
|-------------------|------------|------------|----------------|
| **Guest**         | ❌         | ❌         | ✅             |
| **Authenticated** | ✅         | ❌         | ✅ (own only)  |
| **Admin**         | ✅         | ✅         | ✅             |

---
## 📦 Storage & Quality

- Max upload size: **10MB** per file.
- Supported formats: `.jpg`, `.png`, `.webp`, `.gif`.
- Thumbnails auto-generated.

---

## 🙏 Respect the Community

This platform is built for sharing and discovering. Please respect creators and their rights. Repeated violations may lead to suspension.

---

Feel free to reach out if you have questions or suggestions.

Thanks for contributing to **s3g**!
